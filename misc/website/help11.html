Higher order functions

Higher order functions are easy in MISC. Functions can be passed around as values.

  • [[if false then:+ else:-] 4 5]
  • [let '[+:* f:+] '[+ 5 [f 10 2]]]
  • [let '[ compose:[lambda '[f:1 g:2] '[lambda '[v:1] '[f [g v]]] ] curry:[lambda '[f:1 a:2] '[lambda '[b:1] '[f a b]]] ] '{ A:[[curry + 1] 5] B:[[curry * 4] [[curry + 1] 5]] C:[[compose [curry * 4] [curry + 1]] 5] } ]
